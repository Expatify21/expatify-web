We are ExPatify

Your new best friends in the Netherlands!

EXPATIFY is a brand that aims to help and empower expats as they navigate life in a foreign country. Our mission is to make expats feel connected and at home wherever they are, and our name represents the concept of a life without limits. We hope that our name conveys our concern for foreigners arriving in the Netherlands, as well as our desire to make them feel welcome and supported. Expatify recognizes the unique challenges and opportunities that come with living and working in a new country. As a result, we offer a variety of services tailored specifically to our target market, such as document translation, legalization, and authentication. We also encourage social networking among other expats living abroad.

In a world of international relocation, we believe that moving to a new country should be an exciting adventure, not a stressful paperwork nightmare. Only then can individuals fully embrace and enjoy the experience of living abroad.

We believe in:

  • Safety- We, as expats just like you, know the fear of moving abroad and all the uncertainty it brings with it. That is why, we value safety, and it is our top priority when it comes to helping you with your documents. Our goal is to provide you with the latest information and guide you safely into your journey of moving abroad.
  • Support - For us, it is important to support you in any conditions and problems that might arise during your preliminary stage of moving abroad; that is why we are always one call away!
